Travel

I enjoy exploring new places, from busy cities to quiet hiking trails. Moving from Bangladesh to Oklahoma and now to Connecticut has given me the chance to experience very different cultures, landscapes, and food traditions.

In the future this page will include photos and short stories from my favorite trips—national parks, conferences, and spontaneous weekend adventures.

Cooking

Cooking is one of my favorite ways to relax after the lab. I especially like Bangladeshi home cooking, simple healthy meals for busy days, and experimenting with new ingredients I discover in local markets.

I plan to share some recipes and meal ideas here, focused on practical, tasty food for students and researchers living away from home.
