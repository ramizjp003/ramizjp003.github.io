About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Chemistry & Biochemistry at the University of Oklahoma, with a background in pharmaceutical sciences and industrial quality assurance. My work focuses on antibiotic resistance in Gram-negative bacteria, efflux pump inhibition, and drug discovery.

Outside the lab I enjoy traveling, cooking, and sharing my journey as an international researcher.

Research Interests

  • Drug discovery and development
  • Multidrug efflux pumps and antibiotic resistance
  • Protein–protein and protein–small molecule interactions
  • Biophysical methods and assay development
  • Bioinformatics, machine learning, and deep learning in biology
